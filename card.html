Koala

The word "koala" is thought to originate from one of the Australian Aboriginal languages, which translates to "no drink".

  • Scientific Name: Phascolarctos Cinereus
  • Average Size: 8.5kg-12kg
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Habitat: Open forest and woodland communities

Koalas are often referred to as "koala bears", but this is a misnomer of the past as they are more closely related to kangaroos, bandicoots and possums than to true bears.
